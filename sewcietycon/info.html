Ticket Information

Starting prices for tickets to days 1 and 2 will begin at $40. You may purchase a ticket for either of those individual days for this price or you may purchase a ticket for both of those days at that price. The starting price for day 3 will begin at $30. As the day of the convention nears, prices will steadily increase. The start price will always begin this way every year, however, the increase in price overtime may be subject to change each year.

To receive badges to enter the convention, there are three options:

  • Purchase a ticket in advance in order to have it mailed to you. Note that this option will have a deadline (recommended).
  • Purchase a ticket AFTER the deadline, but rather than getting it mailed, you will be required to pick it up at the South Hall at a specific date (recommended).
  • Purchase a ticket at the door and then receive the badge (not recommended).

Volunteering

As Sew-Ciety Con will be busy, the opportunity for volunteers will arise! Some of the tasks of volunteers include:

  • Security and crowd control
  • Handing out badges
  • Holding signs
  • Setting up and helping booths
  • Cleaning

Anyone who is 18 and above can do this! Simply contact us through our email or social media so that our team can get you on board! Note that there are limited spots and all volunteers must be signed up in advance.

What We Will Offer:

Sew-Ciety Con will offer a variety of services throughout the duration of the event. Vendors with a wide range of products will be present, and additionally, photographers will be in attendance. Furthermore, the South Hall food court will be open, allowing for food options during the day. Janitors and security will also be present.

Why Attend?

Attending Sew-Ciety Con will allow for unique experiences like no other convention. Through our movement, we bring awareness to different fashion subcultures, which can help people develop their own personal style. Additionally, these subcultures allow more people to connect through unique styles, leading to stronger community connections.

Furthermore, we give the chance for people to create better purchasing habits, as Sew-Ciety Con allows people to expose themselves to numerous forms of fashion. The clothing presented, moreover, has a strong emphasis on quality.
