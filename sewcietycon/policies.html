Convention Policies

All attendees, both visitors and registered vendors, are expected to adhere to our policies. Please keep in mind that if you violate any of these rules at any time, you may be subjected to getting escorted off the premises and not permitted to return.

  1. NO weapons of any kind on premises, including model or toy versions. This rule is to prevent any unnecessary misunderstandings and to maintain guest, vendor, and staff safety.
  2. Smoking and e-cigarettes will be permitted only outside the venue in smoking-designated areas
  3. Alcohol and substances will not be permitted within the premises. This rule is to maintain the safety of all people and prevents liability for the company and its people. This rule also helps to reduce the risk of any substances getting on the vendor's product.
  4. Outside food & beverage are permitted, at your own risk. Any accidents, spills, or incidents that may happen to products or people are under the attendee's liability. We, as a company, are not responsible for any damage that may occur due to outside foods & beverages brought in by people. If an accident were to occur to a person or product, attendees are fully subject to compensation if deemed necessary.
  5. Any damage or theft to property, persons, or products will not be tolerated. No damage to product, property, or persons will be allowed. Thieves will be prosecuted in compliance with local law. This is a basic rule of thumb to protect everyone and keep order. Any purposeful harm done to property, persons, or products may lead to legal action and may result in being escorted off the property. There is tolerance for accidental harm, but this may still make you subject to charges or paying compensation.
  6. Be respectful towards everyone and choose your language at your own discretion. We want to keep our environment an accepting, friendly, respectful, and safe community. To maintain this, we must expect respect and tolerance from everyone involved. No slurs or offensive language directed towards any person. Avoid escalation of conflict. If you see a possibly dangerous or unsafe situation, contact security and or staff immediately. See something, say something.
  7. We do not support or endorse unjust pricing of resold items, counterfeits of any sort, or mass-produced and unethical product. We want our convention to continue being a safe space for small businesses, fair pricing, community, and ethical slow fashion. If you are caught violating these rules as a vendor, you will be subject to getting escorted out, and Sew-Ciety is entitled to reclaim all profits made. If you see or suspect a vendor is violating the rules, please contact the company or alert staff. This could make you eligible for a reward if the vendor is found to be violating the rules. Background checks will be conducted on vendors to ensure they reflect our Sew-Ciety mission.
