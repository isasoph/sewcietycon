About Us

Our Mission

We are a group that aims to provide a safe space for creativity and self-expression. Inspired by our love for fashion and DIY activities, such as sewing and thrift-flipping, we aim to create a space for subcultures to thrive. We firmly believe there has been a decline in subcultures (ex. gyaru, v-kei, twee), so we feel it is important to create a space for all kinds of communities!

Our Vision

Our vision is to make Sew-Ciety Con a yearly event in Las Vegas that all fashion enthusiasts look forward to!
